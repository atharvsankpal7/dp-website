Campaign Architecture

4-Phase Marketing
Strategy

Phase 1 — Apr 2026

Awareness Blitz

Build top-of-funnel visibility across Maharashtra's SSC/HSC student base before 12th board exams conclude. Focus on brand recall.

  • Instagram Reels campaign — "Life at ADCET" campus stories
  • Pre-roll ads targeting 17–19 age group in Sangli, Kolhapur, Satara
  • WhatsApp bulk broadcast to 10th/12th student databases
  • Strategic Awareness Program by using traditional and digital methods
Phase 2 — May 2026

Interest & Lead Gen

Capture students actively researching colleges after boards. Drive enquiry form fills and WhatsApp opt-ins during peak decision research.

  • Facebook & Instagram Lead Ads with "Free Counselling Session" hook
  • Program-specific landing pages (AI&DS, Aeronautical, Robotics, CSE)
  • Graphic design creatives — placement stats, NAAC badge, department highlights
  • Reelography content: lab tours, alumni success stories, placement announcements
Phase 3 — Jun to Jul 2026

Conversion & Nurturing

Convert leads into confirmed admissions. Engage parents and students through targeted communication during DTE counselling season.

  • WhatsApp drip campaign — fee structure, scholarship info, hostel details
  • Retargeting ads for website visitors and enquiry form submitters
  • Parent-focused Facebook posts — safety, hostel, infrastructure messaging
  • Open house / virtual campus tour promotion via social media
  • Testimonial Reels from current students and alumni
Phase 4 — Aug to Sep 2026

Retention & Referral

Fill Institute-Level seats after CAP rounds. Activate alumni and student referral networks to complete admissions for AY 2026–27.

  • Direct Admission promotional content for institute-level seats
  • Alumni referral campaign with social media amplification
  • Freshers' week content — excitement posts to attract fence-sitters
  • Local newspaper digital supplements in Sangli district (Web-Portal)
  • Performance review and remarketing optimisation
